GarageAcidLab

GarageAcidLab is a Pure Data patch for making algorithmic acid music. You can play it with a midi controller.

There is an album of music made using these patches called squeakyshoecore.

These patches started life as an RjDj scene.

You will also need s-abstractions and rjlib.

GarageAcidLab is Free Software, licensed under the GPLv.

Check out the patches with bzr like this: bzr co http://mccormick.cx/projects/GarageAcidLab/
